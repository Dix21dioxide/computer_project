Our Aim:

BusMarga aims to solve day-to-day issues that public travelers face, making public transport more efficient and user-friendly. The primary goal of this project is to provide a platform where users can easily find necessary routes for short-distance public transportation and offer a ticket booking system for long-route travel. By focusing primarily on public transport within Kathmandu Valley, BusMarga ensures that users have access to detailed information about various types of public transport facilities available in the valley. Furthermore, it serves as a guide for longer journeys by providing relevant details on intercity routes, schedules, and ticketing options. One of the key features of BusMarga is its ability to provide real-time updates on bus routes, traffic conditions, and arrival times, thereby improving reliability and efficiency. The platform is designed to be accessible to all demographics, including students, elderly passengers, and new users unfamiliar with Kathmandu’s transport system. Through seamless integration with digital payment gateways, BusMarga facilitates easy ticket booking, reducing the hassle of standing in long queues for tickets. The platform also aims to foster a culture of shared mobility by encouraging carpooling and ride-sharing initiatives, further easing traffic congestion in the valley. Moreover, BusMarga seeks to enhance public transport safety by implementing security features such as emergency contact options, feedback mechanisms, and real-time tracking. By addressing key pain points such as lack of information, inefficiency, and safety concerns, BusMarga is set to revolutionize the public transport experience in Kathmandu Valley, making it more convenient, reliable, and inclusive.
